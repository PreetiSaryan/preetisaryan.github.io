Preeti Saryan

I am graduate student at the Department of Biological Sciences, at IISER Bhopal. I am currently working with Dr. Vinita Gowda. I graduated with an integrated Masters degree from IISER Trivandrum with a Major in Biology and a Minor in Chemistry. I worked with Dr. Hema Somanathan for my Master's thesis project, on three sympatric carpenter bee species.

In general I am very fascinated by interactions, how interspecific individuals interact with each other, with other species. Its amazing that every interaction that we come across has some uniqueness in itself. It is wonderful how plants, who can't produce sound and move from place to place for interaction, can still interact with other organisms and attract them to their own accord (of course it is not as easy as it sounds).

As an intern, I have worked on diverse taxa during my undergraduate years. For more on the work I've been part of, refer to the Projects page.
