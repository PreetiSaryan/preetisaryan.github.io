Preeti Saryan

I am graduate student at the Department of Biological Sciences, at IISER Bhopal. I am currently working with Dr. Vinita Gowda. I graduated with an integrated Masters degree from IISER Trivandrum with a Major in Biology and a Minor in Chemistry. I worked with Dr. Hema Somanathan for my Master's thesis project, on temporal niche partitioning in three sympatric carpenter bee species.

In general, I'm fascinated by interactions. Its amazing that every interaction that we come across has some uniqueness in itself. For my PhD, I'm working on the Ecology and Evolution of the genus Hedychium . I am interested in studing the evolutionary ramification of ecology and plant-pollinator interaction on species distribution, speciation and hybrid formation.

I have worked on diverse taxa during my undergraduate years. For more on the work I've been part of, refer to the Projects page.
