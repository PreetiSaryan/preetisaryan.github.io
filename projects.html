Pre-dispersal seed predation of Catunaregam Spinosa:

With Dr. Hema Somanathan* and Dr. Deepak Barua#, I monitored the phenology and fruit infestation in Catunaregam Spinosa. I also studied the pre-dispersal seed predator Deudorix perse to understand it's behaviour. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Temporal flight patters in diurnal & Nocturnal Indian carpenter bees:

I observed three sympatric carpenter bee species in their natural nesting habitat to study their temporal flight patterns, the niche overlap and abiotic factors affecting onset & offset of flight activity as a part of my Masters thesis under the supervision of Dr. Hema Somanathan (School of Biology, IISER-TVM). A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Chemical synthesis & biological studies of beta-D-galactopyranosyl triazoles:

I synthesized Galactose based triazoles using click chemistry and assessed purity and structure of the compounds through NMR. These compounds were further studied for their medicinal properties by subjecting to HeLa cells. This project was part of my Minor project with Dr. K.M. Sureshan (School of Chemistry, IISER-TVM) as a part of Dual-BS-MS degree.. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Vegetation & structural composition of Myristica swamp species:

As part of a larger ongoing project on Myristica swamps, I was involved in characterizing the vegetation composition and structural features of Myristica swamps. I was part of developing the baseline information on the influence of habitat fragmentation on adult species richness and abundance in these swamp forests. I did this work in the lab of Dr. Hema Somanathan as an intern. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Influence of spatial structure on reproductive success of B. Courtallensis:

As an intern in the lab of Dr. Hema Somanathan (IISER-TVM), I looked at the multiple aspects of the reproductive ecology of Baccaurea courtallensis, this included determination of sex ratio, spatial dynamics using point pattern analysis and the effect of neighbouring trees on the fruit-set of females. A manuscript is ensured from this work. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Dietary assessment of two stream dwelling anurans:

Along with Seshadri KS and Dr. T. Ganesh (Ashoka Trust for Research in Ecology and the Environment, Banglore), I studied dietary composition, tropic niche width & overlap on the diets of arthropodivorous anurans, Micrixalus fuscus & Nyctibatrachus cf. vasanthi. Study involved bolus collection from live frogs in Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve, Tamil Nadu and identification of insects in bolus. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.

Use of medicinal flora in the trans-Himalayan ranges:

I compiled a herbarium with the names and uses of various medicinal flora by interviewing the locals in my native area (Kinnaur district, Himachal Pradesh). This study shed light on detrimental effects of agricultural land expansion on hill tops and led me to realize need for conservation. This work was presented during 2011-science exhibition at IISER TVM where I showcase the collected species. A detailed report that discusses the work can be found here.
